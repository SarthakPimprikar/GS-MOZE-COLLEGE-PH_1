=========================================================
GS MOZE COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING - ERP PHASE 1 WORKFLOW
=========================================================

This document outlines the institutional workflows and role-based
permissions for the completed Attendance, Library, and Letter 
Generation modules.

---------------------------------------------------------
1. ADMISSION LIFE-CYCLE
---------------------------------------------------------
PRIMARY ROLES: Student, Admin

[A] STUDENT PHASE:
- Student registers on the portal.
- Fills the comprehensive admission form with academic and personal details.

[B] ADMINISTRATIVE PHASE:
- Admin views incoming applications in 'Enquiries & Leads'.
- Admin verifies the Student's details.
- Admin clicks the 'Approve' button next to the student's name.

[C] SYSTEM RESPONSE:
- Admission bar turns GREEN for the student.
- Welcome Message: "Welcome to GS Moze College, you have been selected..."
- Unique Student/Admission ID is generated and locked.

---------------------------------------------------------
2. LIBRARY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
---------------------------------------------------------
PRIMARY ROLES: Librarian (Staff), Student

[A] LIBRARIAN PHASE:
- Librarian manages the book catalog (Add/Edit/Inventory).
- Librarian issues books using 'Student Email ID' for search.
- Librarian monitors 'Active Issues' for overdue book tracking.

[B] STUDENT PHASE:
- Student searches the 'Catalog' by title, author, or category.
- Student finds shelf locations and availability.
- Student tracks 'My Issued Books' for due dates and live fines.

[C] SYSTEM LOGIC:
- Borrowing Window: Fixed 7-day period.
- Late Fee: ₹50 per day past the due date.
- Fine is calculated automatically during the return process.
- Book stock is updated in real-time upon issue/return.

---------------------------------------------------------
3. LETTER GENERATION & ARCHIVAL
---------------------------------------------------------
PRIMARY ROLES: Admin, Staff

[A] ADMINISTRATIVE PHASE:
- Admin configures HTML templates (Bonafide, LC, Offer Letter).
- Admin/Staff chooses a template based on institutional needs.

[B] ISSUANCE PHASE:
- Staff searches for a recipient (Student/Teacher) by 'Official Email ID'.
- System auto-populates dynamic placeholders (Name, ID, Branch, etc.).
- Staff reviews a live A4-scaled preview of the document.

[C] ARCHIVAL & COMPLIANCE:
- Every letter is assigned a unique Serial Number (GSM/2026/0001).
- Generated letters are permanently archived and available for re-print.
- Digital records cannot be deleted, ensuring a secure institutional audit trail.

---------------------------------------------------------
4. ATTENDANCE & LEAVE MANAGEMENT
---------------------------------------------------------
PRIMARY ROLES: Teacher, Staff, Admin, HOD

[A] RECORDING PHASE:
- Staff/Teachers/HOD mark daily attendance via 'Mark My Attendance'.
- Real-time attendance rate (%) is displayed on their personal dashboard.

[B] LEAVE WORKFLOW:
- User applies for leave using the digital form.
- Admin views all pending leaves in the 'Attendance Overview' portal.
- Admin selects 'Approve' or 'Reject' with real-time toast notifications.

[C] ADMINISTRATIVE OVERSIGHT:
- Admin monitors institutional attendance for all roles in 'Manage Users'.
- Detailed attendance percent is visible when viewing specific profiles.
